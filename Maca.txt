Ingredientes:
1 - macaroni
2 - any type of spice you like
3 - minced meat
4 - vegetable
5 - oil
6 - water

Directions:
1) Boil the macaroni 100% hot water and wash away the starch

2) spice and fry the minced meat with a litte oil

3) mix the minced meat with the vegetable and let it fry for about five(5)min with a low heat

4) Add the macaroni in and mix it together with the vegetable and meat and let it heat for about 10min
